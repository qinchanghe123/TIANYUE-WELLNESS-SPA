FAQs

Before your visit.

A few helpful answers so you can arrive relaxed and ready for your session.

Please arrive about 10 minutes early, especially for your first visit. This gives you time to check in, choose your service, and settle in comfortably.

If you have an injury, recent surgery, or medical concern, please consult your healthcare provider first and let us know before your session so we can help you choose a comfortable option.

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call us as early as possible so we can make the appointment time available for another guest.

Parking is typically available near the spa location at 6600 Manatee Ave W, Bradenton, FL 34209. Please allow a little extra time during busy hours.
